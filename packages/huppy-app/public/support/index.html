Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I connect my computer? Open Huppy on your phone, then scan the QR code shown by the Huppy desktop or CLI flow, or paste the terminal link manually.
  • Do I need to pay to create an account? No. Huppy account access is free. A paid subscription is optional and only unlocks premium features.
  • Where do I buy or restore Huppy Plus? On iPhone and iPad, Huppy Plus is purchased and restored through Apple's in-app purchase flow.
  • What does Huppy Plus unlock? Huppy Plus unlocks premium voice usage beyond the free allowance and allows more than one connected computer per account.
  • Which AI providers does Huppy support? Huppy currently supports Claude Code, Codex, Gemini, and OpenClaw sessions running on your own machine.
  • How do I report a bug? Email us with steps to reproduce the issue, what you expected to happen, and any screenshots that help explain the problem.
