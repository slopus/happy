Our mission

The best tools come from scratching your own itch — then sharing it with everyone.

We're engineers scattered across Bay Area coffee shops and hacker houses, constantly checking how our AI coding agents are doing on our pet projects during lunch breaks. Happy was born from the frustration of not being able to peek at our agents building our side hustles while away from the keyboard. No VCs required — just an itch worth scratching.
